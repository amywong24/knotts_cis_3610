At Knott's, fun is always in season, with dozens of thrilling rides, shows, and attractions in four themed areas inspired by the history and culture of California.


About Knott's Berry Farm

Located just 10 minutes from the Anaheim resort area, the once small family farm has grown into today's family fun destination; home to the Knott's Berry Farm theme park, Knott's Soak City Waterpark and Knott's Hotel. Whether you are looking for a weekend getaway, family road trip, or overnight stay, you’ll find something for everyone at Knott’s Berry Farm!

Activities

Rides & Experiences

Fun is always in season at Knott’s Berry Farm! With dozens of rides, shows and attractions in four themed areas, there is something for everyone at Knott’s. Splash down the Timber Mountain Log Ride. The Boardwalk area offers rides and attractions for the whole family. Stroll along the streets of the Old West Ghost Town® where you’ll meet its many residents. Camp Snoopy, home of the PEANUTS Gang, will round out your day with pint-sized adventures in a high Sierra setting.

Dining

Since the beginning, food and dining has been at the heart of Knott’s Berry Farm. Before there was a theme park, Knott’s was a real berry farm where the boysenberry was first successfully grown. In 1934 Mrs. Knott’s Chicken Dinner Restaurant first opened with Mrs. Knott and her children serving the first customers in their tea room. The restaurant has grown over the years into one of the largest in California, but the classic fried chicken dinner is still made the same way today using Mrs. Knott's recipes. What started as a small family berry farm soon began to grow into a world famous destination thanks to famous fried chicken dinners and delicious boysenberry pies. That culinary innovation still thrives today at Knott’s. Our team of chefs is always striving to bring you new flavors and tasty creations.

Shows and Live Entertainment

The rides are only half the fun! With comedy, live bands, musicals, and more, Knott's offers must-see live entertainment for every age group. The Camp Snoopy Theatre plays host to a number of fun family activities and shows starring Charlie Brown, Lucy, Linus, and the world’s most popular beagle, Snoopy. Plan a visit to Knott's historic, open-air Wagon Camp Theatre, home to a variety of family-friendly entertainment!

Visit Knott's Soak City Waterpark

Knott’s Soak City Waterpark is a separately gated 15-acre water adventure park immediately adjacent to Knott’s Berry Farm. Soak City boasts 23 speed, tube and body slides including Shore Break featuring 6 different waterslide experiences and a thrilling family raft ride, The Wedge; the 750,000-gallon Tidal Wave Bay wavepool; a one‑third‑mile Sunset River; and the three‑story Beach House, featuring 200 water guns, nozzles, sprayers and other interactive surprises.
